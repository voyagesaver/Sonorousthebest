Booking Policy

To ensure the best experience, please take your time and properly read the terms and conditions upon booking!

DEPOSIT

A deposit is required upon booking your appointment, all deposits go towards the final cost of hairstyle chosen.

REMAINING BALANCE

The remaining balance is to be paid in cash or via Zelle on the day of your appointment.

HAIR PRICE

Price includes hair. You can bring your own hair at your cost but, (hair must be pre-stretched).

CLEAN HAIR

Hair must be clean and product free (washed no more than two days) it must be thoroughly detangled.

KEEP IT SAFE

It is imperative for clients to have their FACE MASK on and SANITIZE their hands upon entering the salon

NO REFUND

Deposit is non-refundable, it can be transferred for rescheduling if the appointment is canceled within 48 hours of your initial appointment.

FEELING SICK?

If you have ANY symptoms, please be considerate and reschedule * allergies included

PATIENCE

Please be patient, hairstyles may take take longer on a client's hair than another based on hair texture.

BOOK IN ADVANCE

Booking is allowed up to 60 days out from the current date.

NO EXTRA PEOPLE

Please come to your appointment alone, no EXEMPTION, we can’t risk being crowded.
